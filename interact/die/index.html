So many gasps, but no way to breathe.
Go under, distress, it’s a losing fight.
Scream, useless, only overwhelming silence.

The waters rise, it’s not an easy fight
when pressure and stress take advantage of the silence
and thoughts and voices get too loud, and it gets harder to breathe.

Suppress the thoughts, suppress the silence.
It’ll get better - It’s okay, just breathe.
Don't get swept away, don’t give up the fight.
Take a deep breath - hold - then fight to escape, to shatter the silence, to breathe.

Michelle Yang 2020
